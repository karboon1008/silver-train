Gestures & Microinteractions — Unchanged
All gestures from the previous version still apply. The trip list adds two new ones.
Swipe left on card
→
Reveals Delete / Archive actions under the card. Red for delete, grey for archive. Spring-back on cancel.
Tap trip card
→
Shared element hero transition — the card image morphs into the map hero at the top of Trip Detail. 350ms spring.
FAB tap (+)
→
FAB expands into a modal sheet with two options: "Build manually" / "Generate with AI". Backdrop blur appears.
✦ AI suggest btn
→
Quietly sits at the bottom of the day editor. Tap → shimmer skeleton rows appear, then real suggestions fade in staggered.
Expand map ↗
→
Map hero expands to full-screen with a scale-up spring. All itinerary pins appear as numbered markers. Tap pin → activity detail.
