Feature Breakdown
1
Image icon beside name — Small 32×32 thumbnail (rounded square) pulled from the place's photo. Falls back to a category emoji if no photo available.
2
Category badge — Pill tag below name: 🏨 Accommodation · 🛍️ Mall · 🚉 Station · 🏛️ Landmark · 🍽️ Restaurant · 🌿 Nature · 🎭 Entertainment. Sourced from Google Places type.
3
Editable remark — Tappable text field below category. Pencil (✎) icon on the left signals editability. Saves to Supabase on blur. Empty state shows "Add a remark…" in muted italic.
4
Transport rows — Walk, Metro, Bus, Taxi shown with mode icon, line/route label, and estimated duration from the previous itinerary stop. Times fetched from Google Directions API.
5
Time tap → map redirect — Tapping the blue time pill opens a bottom sheet listing installed map apps (Apple Maps, Google Maps, Waze, Grab). Selection deep-links with the destination's lat/lng and selected transport mode pre-filled.
